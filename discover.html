Discover

I chose the University of Minnesota Duluth (UMD) for several important reasons:

Affordability — UMD Duluth offers competitive tuition rates and financial aid options that made a quality education financially accessible.
Excellent Business School — The Labovitz School of Business and Economics is highly regarded and provides strong coursework, networking opportunities, and career support for aspiring business professionals.
Overall Educational Quality — UMD Duluth is a respected public university with a strong reputation for academics, research, and student success across many disciplines.
Love for Duluth — The city itself is amazing. Duluth has a vibrant culture, great community, and is located in one of the most beautiful regions in Minnesota.
Nature & Parks — Duluth is surrounded by incredible natural beauty and has fantastic parks for hiking, outdoor activities, and exploring. It's a paradise for anyone who loves the outdoors.
Lake Superior — Being right on Lake Superior is incredible. The lake offers stunning views, recreational opportunities, and a unique connection to nature that drew me to UMD Duluth.

My time at UMD Duluth has been instrumental in shaping my career interests, especially in sales and marketing, and I've had great opportunities to grow both academically and personally.
