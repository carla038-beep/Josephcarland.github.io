Hobbies

I love to play sports, enjoy being in nature, and I'm active in Ultimate Frisbee at UMD.

Ultimate Frisbee — I play Ultimate Frisbee for the club team here at UMD. I started playing the sport for the first time during my freshman year, and it has been an amazing experience. Ultimate is a great way to make new friends, stay active, and have a lot of fun competing with my teammates. My favorite part is going on tournaments — every year we travel down to Iowa for a tournament, and it's so much fun to hang out with the team at the hotel, playing video games and bonding with teammates. One memorable moment was last year when we played against Wisconsin Milwaukee, a very athletic and physical team that's super hard to play against. We played the best we had in a long time and ended up beating them 15-11, which was an incredible achievement. I love the sport and the community that comes with it.
Hiking & Outdoors — I really enjoy hiking and being outdoors. I love camping, kayaking, rock climbing, and exploring all of the trails that Duluth has to offer. My favorite place to hike in Duluth is Jay Cooke State Park. Last year, my friend and I camped there for a weekend, and we spent the time hiking, fishing, and cooking food. It was an amazing experience surrounded by nature. The natural beauty of the area provides endless opportunities for adventure and outdoor exploration.
Casual Games — I enjoy playing games with my friends like spikeball, Settlers of Catan, and disc golf. In the summer, my friends and I will go to the park and spend the day playing touch football, spikeball, frisbee, and other outdoor games. These activities are a great way to hang out, have fun, enjoy some friendly competition, and spend time bonding with each other as friends.
