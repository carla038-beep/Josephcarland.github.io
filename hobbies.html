Hobbies

I love to play sports, enjoy being in nature, and I'm active in Ultimate Frisbee at UMD.

Ultimate Frisbee — I play Ultimate Frisbee for the club team here at UMD. I started playing the sport for the first time during my freshman year, and it has been an amazing experience. Ultimate is a great way to make new friends, stay active, and have a lot of fun competing with my teammates. I love the sport and the community that comes with it.
Hiking & Outdoors — I really enjoy hiking and being outdoors. I love camping, kayaking, rock climbing, and exploring all of the trails that Duluth has to offer. The natural beauty of the area provides endless opportunities for adventure and outdoor exploration.
Casual Games — I enjoy playing games with my friends like spikeball, Settlers of Catan, and disc golf. These activities are a great way to hang out, have fun, and enjoy some friendly competition.
